Looking for a job

  • Reading
  • Questions

Currently, Julian lives in New York City. He takes the subway to go to his school every day. Today he is going to fill an application for a job. He had to find lots of information about himself: where he lives, his address, telephone number, email account; what he is good at, that is, his abilities; and a very important area: studies and his work experience or his previous jobs.

All this information has to be placed in a document called: Resume. Whenever you are going to fill a job application, they will always ask you for your Resume.

Contesta la preguntas correctamente.

1. Where does Julian live?

Wrong answer, try again Correct answer!

2. Does he take the train or the bus to go to his school?

Wrong answer, try again Correct answer!

3. What is he going to do today?

Wrong answer, try again Correct answer!

4. What kind of information did he have to find?

Wrong answer, try again Correct answer!

5. What is a resume?

Wrong answer, try again Correct answer!
