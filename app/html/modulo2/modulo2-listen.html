Talking on the phone

Sandy: Hi, Martha? It’s me. Look, we’re having trouble with the sink.
 Martha:Oh really? Where are you?
 Sandy:I’m in the kitchen, fixing my sink. Where are you?.
 Martha: I’m in the living room, watching T.V.
 Sandy: Where are Mr. and Mrs. Black?
 Martha:They are in the yard. Where is your husband?.

Sandy: She’s in the basement looking for some tools.
Martha: Oh! You’re busy. I’ll call you tomorrow.
Sandy:Thank you. Have a good day. Bye, bye.
