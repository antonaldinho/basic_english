Try writing a friendly letter. First, read the example. Then, try to write a letter. / Ahora practica escribiendo una carta amistosa. Primero lee el ejemplo, después, trata de escribir una carta.

Dear Juanita,

How are you? And the children? I hope you are well. We are all fine here. Our life in America is difficult, but we like it very much. I have a job at Miguelito's school. It is a bilingual school and they teach him English every day. I help the teacher. Martha is learning English, too. She likes her new school and her new classmates.

Roberto is working with his cousin, Luis. He builds houses. The money is good, but it is difficult to communicate with everyone. I don’t speak English and sometimes I get frustrated because I can’t understand everyone. Martha helps me. I hope to hear from you soon.

I love you all and miss you very much. We have a new telephone number. It is (607)894-9984.

Call me when you get my letter. Say hello to everyone.

Love,
Angelina.
